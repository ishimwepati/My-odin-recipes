Descirption

This is african beans recipe, the one eaten with Chapaties, in Uganda it is called KIKOMANDO

Ingrediens

  • Chili
  • Oil
  • Tomatoes
  • Salt
  • Galic
  • Oignons

Step by Step

  1. Prepare your Beans
  2. Boil your beans for 2 to 3 hour
  3. Dry your beans and prepare your Oil
  4. Once Oil is ready mix with Oignons, Tomatoes and Galic
